            // command-line first
            
A workflow built for the keyboard.

            

              Runs as a single CLI app backed by a local SQLite database for your watchlist,
              wealth, budgets, preferences, and models. Set a default ticker so commands can
              omit the symbol — everything stays on your machine.
            

          


          

            

              ghost watch
              
Track your watchlist of saved tickers in real time.

            

            

              ghost quote
              
Pull a live snapshot for any stock, future, or forex pair.

            

            

              ghost wealth
              
Manage personal finances, budgets, accounts, and transactions.

            

            

              ghost macro
              
Track rates, forex, futures, and broad macro indicators.

            

            

              ghost orb
              
Multi-agent AI research across your markets & finances.

            

            

              ghost models install
              
Install local models like llama3.2, gpt-oss, or gemma.

            // security & ownership
            
It lives on your machine. Period.

            

              Ghost has no servers, no accounts, and no telemetry. Everything you do stays in a local,
              encrypted SQLite database that only you control — radical transparency by design.
            

          

          

            

              

                
              

              
Encrypted SQLite

              
Your financial data is encrypted at rest in a local database — secured on your disk, not someone else's.

            

            

              

                
              

              
Zero cloud, zero accounts

              
No sign-up, no sync, no tracking. Ghost runs entirely offline-first — you never hand over your data.

            

            

              

                
              

              
Total transparency

              
You can see, export, and delete everything at any time. It's your machine, your database, your rules.

            

          

        

      


      
      

        

          

            

              
              Ask Orb
            

          

          

            // multi-agent ai research
            
Meet the Orb.

            

              The Orb runs a multi-agent mode set up to understand every aspect of your finances and
              investments — markets, wealth, budgets, and macro — then summarizes, analyzes, and answers
              your questions in plain English. Run it entirely on local models so nothing ever leaves your machine.
            

            

              Install local models:
              ghost models install llama3.2 gpt-oss gemma
